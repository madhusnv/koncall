# KonCall CRM - Feature Roadmap

A comprehensive feature roadmap to enhance KonCall into a full-fledged CRM app with monetization potential.

---

## üéØ Phase 1: Essential CRM Features (High Priority)

### 1. Lead Filters & Search
- Filter by **stage** (New, Contacted, Interested, Enrolled, Joined)
- Filter by **date range** (today, this week, this month, custom)
- Filter by **assignee** (my leads vs team leads)
- Full-text **search** by name, phone, email
- **Sort** by last contacted, created date, follow-up date

### 2. Follow-up Reminders
- Set reminder date/time for each lead
- Push notifications for due follow-ups
- "Today's Tasks" dashboard widget
- Overdue follow-ups alert with visual indicators
- Snooze and reschedule options

### 3. Lead Assignment & Team Management
- Assign leads to team members
- View "My Assigned Leads" vs "All Leads"
- Admin can reassign leads
- Lead transfer history with audit trail
- Team hierarchy (Manager ‚Üí Callers)

### 4. Notes & Activity Timeline
- Timestamped notes on each lead
- Activity history (calls, stage changes, notes)
- Quick note popup after each call *(existing feature)*
- Attachments support (documents, images)
